Overview

Emerging domains like 5G, MEC, SDN-NFV,etc. are driving the shift towards cloud-native future networks with increasingly disaggregating components, both proprietary and open-source.

The resulting technological diversity of the hybrid overall network estate makes this transformation complex, expensive but critical.

46%Reduction in rework through service delivery transformation

15k+devices benefit from reduced downtime and lesser reboots due to IP transformation

Prodapt Software Intensive Networks Framework is a portfolio toolkit of interlocking services and solutions that combine IT, NT, and Cloud technologies. It accelerates network transformation value through E2E solutions across evolving networks and their control systems. With our Software Intensive Networks Framework, Telecom, Entertainment and Tech companies can accelerate their 5G go-to-market initiatives, enable new digital revenues, and reduce TCO.

5G a world of new opportunities to service providers. However, these benefits will not be realised if networks are not upto the task at hand.

Rajiv Papneja | Global Head - Network Cloud Services
