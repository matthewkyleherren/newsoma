PRODAPT FOUNDATION

Do good, feel good. Get involved in social responsibility

We are on a sustainable endeavour towards ‘being the change we would like to see in the world.’

Objective With a workforce passionate to contribute to the cause of creating a better society, Prodapt strives to fulfil its social responsibilities by participating in community activities–big and small. As a socially responsible global organization, we are committed to addressing various social issues by leveraging “the 3 Ts” – Time, Talent & Technology.

We are focussed on creating impact on education for underprivileged, health care and conserving environment. We are on a sustainable endeavour towards ‘being the change we would like to see in the world.’

Philosophy Prodapt Foundation – a non-profit unit of Prodapt aimed at the welfare of the society, is built on the company’s core values – Persistence, Futuristic, Depth, and Speed guided by best practices and driven by our aspiration for excellence.
